Showreel

Showreel Motion designer specialising in expressive, character-driven animation and crisp, engaging motion graphics. I work across tools like Rive and After Effects to create interactive and linear animations that feel playful, purposeful, and polished. My work blends personality-led character animation with clean, snappy motion design—bringing ideas to life through timing, rhythm, and attention to detail. Whether it's micro-interactions, explainer visuals, or brand-led animation, I focus on making motion that not only looks good, but communicates clearly and feels great to experience.

Bolt 6 For this project, I added interactive motion design to Bolt6's website to make the introduction of its six founding members more engaging. I illustrated each founder in my own style and turned the portraits into animated, interactive "mugshots" that appeared throughout the site, bringing a playful edge to a typically serious brand. A key success was how naturally the illustrations fit the existing design while adding personality and humour. The main challenge was balancing creativity with professionalism, ensuring the animations felt expressive yet on-brand. The final result made the site more memorable, human, and visually engaging — achieving exactly what Bolt6 wanted. bolt6.ai/about-us
