Client 44 Pixels At 44 Pixels, I worked as a Motion Designer and Creative Technologist, helping shape the user experience across a suite of AI-driven products including Clara (an AI receptionist for craftsmen), VIVI (a tool that turns long-form video into viral clips), Cue (an AI meeting note taker), and WordCast (a text-to-speech platform). My focus was on designing and developing interactive onboarding experiences using Rive and other real-time motion tools. I combined motion design with logic-driven systems such as data binding and event-based triggers to create adaptive, multilingual onboarding flows that could respond dynamically to user input and behaviour. This approach allowed onboarding journeys to be personalised, translated, and interactive—reacting to touch and user engagement in real time. I also built quiz-based flows and embedded analytics events to help the team better understand user behaviour, including drop-off points, engagement patterns, and user segmentation. My background in motion design gave me a strong foundation in crafting engaging, intuitive interactions that improved user retention and clarity. The work sat at the intersection of design, animation, and product thinking—where storytelling, usability, and data all came together to shape more effective user experiences.
